Pink Drink

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberry açai refresher
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • Freeze-dried strawberries

Instructions

  1. Fill glass with ice
  2. Pour in strawberry açai
  3. Top with coconut milk

Caramel Macchiato

Ingredients

  • 2 shots espresso
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp vanilla syrup
  • Caramel sauce

Instructions

  1. Add vanilla syrup to glass
  2. Pour milk over ice
  3. Slowly add espresso

Mocha Frappuccino

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strong coffee (chilled)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 2 cups ice

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients
  2. Pour into glass
  3. Top with whipped cream

Pro Tip

Use chilled glasses for iced drinks!
